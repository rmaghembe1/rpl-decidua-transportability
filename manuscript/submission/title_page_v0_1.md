## Main claim

A GSE214607-derived recurrent-miscarriage decidual inflammatory macrophage/APC programme shows concordant positive directional recurrence across three independent bulk decidua cohorts: GSE161969, GSE113790 and GSE178535.

## Evidence boundary

The study supports exploratory programme-level transportability. It does not establish causality, diagnostic performance, clinical utility or standalone biomarker validation.
